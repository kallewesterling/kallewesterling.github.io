<p>I have collected 70 visualizations of New York City, that <em>mostly</em> build on open data. The qualification for a visualization to make it on this list was to be interactive in one way or another. Some of them are not very advanced, using Carto's standard mapping technology, and others are not available online but were constructed as art exhibitions. The latter ones are included because of their extensive documentation in the links below.</p>
